## What to Cover
- **Never Trust, Always Verify** — every request authenticated regardless of origin
- **Least Privilege** — users/devices only get what they need
- **Assume Breach** — design as if attackers are already in; micro-segmentation
- **Continuous Monitoring** — real-time telemetry, behavioral analytics, dynamic policies

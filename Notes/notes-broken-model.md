## Checklist

### A. How Perimeter Security Works (keep this short — 1 paragraph)
- [ ] Castle-and-moat analogy: firewall = drawbridge, once inside = trusted

### B. The Assumptions That No Longer Hold
- [ ] Cloud moved data outside the castle
- [ ] Remote work moved users outside

### C. The Evidence (this is your strongest sub-section — be specific)
